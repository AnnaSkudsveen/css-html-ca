Our history

Pushing the Comfort Zone

1954 - The Founding Years

In 1954, RainyDays was founded by outdoor enthusiasts who had a vision of creating high-quality outdoor clothing and gear. With a small workshop and a dedication to craftsmanship, they set out to serve the needs of adventurers and nature lovers.

1962 - Expanding Product Line

In the early 1960s, RainyDays expanded its product line to include a wider range of outdoor clothing. This expansion allowed the company to cater to the growing demand from outdoor enthusiasts who sought durable and functional gear.

1976 - Retail Store Opening

In 1976, RainyDays opened its first retail store. This marked a significant milestone, as it allowed more customers to experience the brand's commitment to quality and service in person. The store quickly became a hub for outdoor enthusiasts.

1982 - Sustainable Initiatives

In the early 1980s, RainyDays began incorporating sustainable practices into its manufacturing processes. This included using eco-friendly materials and reducing waste. These efforts were ahead of their time and reflected the company's commitment to the environment.

1999 - E-commerce Launch

With the advent of the internet, RainyDays embraced e-commerce in 1999. The launch of its online store made its products accessible to a global audience, further cementing its position as a leading outdoor clothing brand.

2006 - Technical Innovation

In 2006, RainyDays introduced innovative technical features into its clothing and gear. This included waterproof and breathable materials, ergonomic designs, and cutting-edge technology. These advancements enhanced the outdoor experience for customers.

2010 - Community Engagement

RainyDays prioritized community engagement in 2010. The company sponsored outdoor events, supported conservation efforts, and encouraged its customers to get involved in nature-related initiatives. This commitment to community strengthened its bond with customers who shared its passion for the outdoors.

2020 - Sustainable Future

In 2020, RainyDays reaffirmed its dedication to sustainability. The company launched a comprehensive sustainability program, aiming to reduce its carbon footprint, increase the use of recycled materials, and support environmental causes. This forward-thinking approach positioned RainyDays as a leader in eco-conscious outdoor apparel.
